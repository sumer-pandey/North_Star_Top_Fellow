# Market Gap — India E-Learning

## Current Weaknesses
- Coursera/edX: high cost, <10% completion ([source](https://reports.weforum.org/docs/WEF_Future_of_Jobs_Report_2025.pdf))  
- Unacademy: exam/test prep focus  
- upGrad: expensive, long programs  
- Scaler: niche (CS only), high cost  
- YouTube: free but no structure, no accountability  

## Gaps Identified
1. Consistency → completion  
2. Transparent ROI & salary uplift data  
3. Personalized, ROI-first roadmaps  
4. Gamified streaks + focus sessions  
5. Affordable, Tier-2/3 friendly  

📌 *Sources: NASSCOM, Kantar 2023; World Bank, ASER Survey; IBEF.*
