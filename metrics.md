# Metrics — North Star

## North Star Metric (NSM)
**Verified Learning Weeks (VLW):** ≥4 Focus sessions (≥10m) + ≥1 quiz/project per week.  
*(Backed by behavioral science research on habit loops — Charles Duhigg, BJ Fogg)*

## Leading Indicators
- Onboarding completion ≥80%  
- Time-to-ROI view ≤30s  
- Shortlist CTR ≥55%  
- D1 activation ≥70%  
- D3 streak ≥50%  
- Focus sessions/week ≥4  
- Avg focus minutes = 12–15 min  
- Quiz completion ≥65%  
- WA/Calendar opt-in ≥70%  

## Lagging Indicators
- Completion = 35–40% (vs <10% MOOC avg, Coursera baseline [source](https://www.coursera.org/))  
- Mini-project submissions ≥40%  
- Certificate views ≥60%  
- Free→Paid = 8–12%  
- Repeat ≥20%  
- Referral = 5–7%  
- ARPU ↑ trend
